TrustFund is a centralized digital scholarship management system designed to simplify and streamline the entire scholarship lifecycle for both applicants and administrators. The platform allows students to easily apply for scholarships by filling out structured forms, uploading required documents, and tracking the status of their applications in real time. This eliminates the need for manual submissions and reduces delays associated with traditional processes.

On the administrative side, TrustFund provides a powerful dashboard for managing applications efficiently. Administrators can review submissions, verify applicant information, shortlist candidates based on predefined criteria, and approve or reject applications with ease. The system also supports document validation and helps ensure that only eligible candidates receive funding.

In addition, TrustFund enhances transparency and accountability in the allocation of scholarships. Applicants receive timely updates on their application progress, while administrators can generate detailed reports and analytics on application trends, fund distribution, and overall system performance. This data-driven approach supports better decision-making and resource allocation.

The system integrates with **M-Pesa's STK Push (Lipa Na M-Pesa Online API)** to facilitate secure, seamless, and trackable disbursement of funds to successful applicants. Once a scholarship is approved, funds are sent directly to the applicant's registered M-Pesa number via an STK Push prompt, eliminating the need for manual bank transfers or physical cheque collections. The integration ensures:

- **Real-time payment processing** – Applicants receive funds instantly upon approval.
- **Transaction tracking** – Every disbursement is logged and can be verified by administrators.
- **Reduced fraud risks** – Funds go directly to verified mobile numbers linked to applicant accounts.
- **Automated reconciliation** – Payment statuses are automatically updated in the system.

With role-based access control, TrustFund ensures that sensitive data is protected while allowing different users—such as students, reviewers, and administrators—to interact with the system according to their roles.

Overall, TrustFund improves efficiency, reduces administrative workload, minimizes errors and fraud, and provides a user-friendly experience for all stakeholders involved in scholarship management.
